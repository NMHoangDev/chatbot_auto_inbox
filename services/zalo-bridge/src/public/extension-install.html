Tai tien ich Markee Zalo

Tien ich nay chi dung de mo Zalo Web, lay phien dang nhap cua dung tai khoan Zalo ca nhan va gui ve Markee Chat. Sau khi ket noi xong, nguoi dung thao tac trong Markee Chat.

Tai file cai dat ZIP
1

Tai va giai nen ZIP

Tai file ZIP o nut tren, sau do giai nen ra mot thu muc co ten de nho, vi du markee-zalo-extension.

2

Mo trang tien ich Chrome

Mo Chrome, vao chrome://extensions, bat cong tac Developer mode o goc tren ben phai.

3

Chon Load unpacked

Bam Load unpacked va chon dung thu muc da giai nen o buoc 1.

4

Quay lai Markee Chat

Tai lai trang ket noi Zalo ca nhan trong Markee Chat, roi bam Dang nhap tai khoan Zalo.

Ban cai dat noi bo nay dung cho giai doan test. De khach hang khong can bat Developer mode, nen publish tien ich len Chrome Web Store va cau hinh Markee tro den link cai dat chinh thuc.
